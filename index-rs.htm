    Religious Zionists in Jerusalem
    Between 70 and 75 percent of the Jewish population of Jerusalem identify with Jewish religious tradition in some significant way. Many of those defining themselves as traditional are of Sephardic background, and most are periodic synagogue attenders and maintain home Sabbath and holiday observances with regularity.
